Website Planning Document

Audience

Our target audience is locals and visitors. We want locals to be able to come to our site and feel a part of the community. We have evnets going on all year round and we want our citizens to come and join us as we have these different events. We also want visitors to be able to feel welcome and easily be able to accomadate their travel plans with whatever events we have going on during their time with us.

Persona 1

Jonah

Occupation: Small entrepreneur that works in Web Design.

Demographics: Age: 26. Makes enough money to go out and do fun things with his family and friends. Enjoys doing activities indoors and playing games. He wants to be able to do more activities in his community.

Goals: Jonah wants to get to know his fellow citizens more so that he can be a part of community events more.

Persona 2

Jane

Occupation: Comic book store owner.

Demographics: Age: 50. Enjoys spending time with her two kids and her husband. Wants to be able to grow her business a little bit more by getting to know the community around her.

Goals: Jane wants to connect with her area more so she can get more customers and also learn about other small town businesses in her area.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What's the weather going to be like this week for my hike at Island Falls?

Scenario 2: Are there any events I should know about going on this weekend?

Scenario 3: What small businesses can I connect with at the local street fair?

Scenario 4: Where is the map for the city?

Scenario 5: What time is the local farmer's market this weekend?
