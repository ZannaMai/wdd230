Website Planning Document

Audience

Our target audience is locals and visitors. We want locals to be able to come to our site and feel a part of the community. We have evnets going on all year round and we want our citizens to come and join us as we have these different events. We also want visitors to be able to feel welcome and easily be able to accomadate their travel plans with whatever events we have going on during their time with us.

Persona 1

John

Occupatiion: Small entrepreneur selling fishing lures.

Demographics: Age: 26. Makes about $10k per year as an entrepreneur and $40k working other jobs. Has a high school diploma. Loves to go fishing and hunting. Is married and has 2 little kids. He mostly uses his phone to make sales and to promote his business because it is always with him.

Goals: John wants to grow his business and so that eventually he can sell his fishing lures and make enough to support his family without having to work extra jobs.

Persona 2

Jane

Occupation: Restaurant owner.

Demographics: Age: 50. Makes about $80k per year. Has a bachlors degree and is married with 3 teenage children. She mostly uses her computer to track sales and find what she needs.

Goals: Jane wants to connect with more businesses around her. She wants to be able to help them out as well as learn how they might be able to help her.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What's the weather going to be like this week for my hike at Antelope Island?

Scenario 2: Are there any interesting things going on this weekend in Syracuse?

Scenario 3: What new store is going up in Syracuse?

Scenario 4: What time does the city parade start?

Scenario 5: How can I connect with other local businesses?
