White Space and Clean Design

Family Search

familysearch.org

Family Search is a good example of using white space and having a clean design because it utilizes it in a way were you know where their important content is. I enjoy this site because it doesn't bombard you with a lot of information, but it gives you what is most important and guides you easily where you want to go.

PARC: Contrast

Lume Homepage

lume.com

Lume is a website for natural deodorant that I have come across and I remember it mostly because of its use of colors on their website. I believe this website has good contrast because the colors allow the reader to easily read what is on the page and it allows grabs the users attention easily with the color pallette.

PARC: Proximity

Youtube

youtube.com

Youtube is an easy choice for proximity. This website is all about video content so they know that it is importamt to out things close together, but also make it so the user can easily choose where they want to go. Their buttons aren't far apart so you can choose what sort of ocntent you want to see and the search bar is also close by so the user can easily search for the content that they want to see.
